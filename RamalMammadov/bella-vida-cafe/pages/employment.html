Why Work at Bella Vida Garden Cafe?

At Bella Vida Garden Cafe, we believe that our team is the heart of our success. We're looking for passionate individuals who share our values of hospitality, quality, and community service.

Employee Benefits

  • Competitive wages with opportunities for advancement
  • Flexible scheduling to accommodate your lifestyle
  • Employee meal discounts
  • Positive, family-oriented work environment
  • Training and development opportunities
  • Health and wellness programs
  • Paid time off for eligible employees
  • Employee recognition and rewards

Server

Part-time & Full-time

Join our front-of-house team and provide exceptional service to our guests. Experience preferred but not required - we provide training!

  • Greet and serve guests with warmth and professionalism
  • Knowledge of menu items and ingredients
  • Maintain clean and organized work areas
  • Work collaboratively with kitchen staff

Kitchen Staff

Part-time & Full-time

Help prepare delicious, wholesome meals in our kitchen. Perfect opportunity for those passionate about food and quality.

  • Prepare menu items according to recipes
  • Maintain food safety and sanitation standards
  • Assist with food prep and plating
  • Work in a fast-paced environment

Barista

Part-time

Create perfect coffee and espresso drinks for our guests. Coffee experience preferred but training provided.

  • Prepare coffee, espresso, and specialty drinks
  • Maintain coffee equipment and cleanliness
  • Provide excellent customer service
  • Knowledge of coffee origins and brewing methods

Host/Hostess

Part-time

  • Greet guests and manage seating
  • Answer phones and manage reservations

  • Assist with takeout orders
