Our Story

The heart and soul of Bella Vida Garden Cafe

The Monge Family Legacy

Bella Vida Garden Cafe was born from a dream that began in the heart of the Monge family. Our journey started with a simple vision: to create a place where people could gather, share meals, and create memories in an environment filled with love, faith, and wholesome food.

Meet Our Family

Maria Monge

Founder & Head Chef

Maria's passion for cooking began in her grandmother's kitchen. With over 20 years of culinary experience, she brings traditional family recipes and modern techniques to create our signature dishes.

Carlos Monge

Co-Owner & Manager

Carlos oversees the daily operations and ensures every guest receives exceptional service. His dedication to customer satisfaction is the foundation of our hospitality.

Our Journey

2018

The Dream Begins

The Monge family purchased the property on 406 Broadway with a vision to create a community gathering place.

2019

Renovation & Planning

Months of careful renovation transformed the space into a beautiful garden cafe with indoor and outdoor seating.

2020

Grand Opening

Bella Vida Garden Cafe opened its doors to the West Cape May community, serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

2024

Growing Strong

Today, we continue to serve our community with the same love and dedication that inspired us from the beginning.
