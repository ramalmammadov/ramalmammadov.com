Our Story

The heart and soul of Bella Vida Garden Cafe

A Family Legacy

Bella Vida Garden Cafe was born from the Monge family's passion for creating wholesome, delicious food in a warm, welcoming environment. Our journey began with a simple dream: to serve our community with love, faith, and the finest ingredients.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of good food to bring people together. Every dish we serve is crafted with care, using fresh, local ingredients whenever possible. Our faith-based approach guides everything we do, from how we treat our customers to how we source our ingredients.

Wholesome & Healthy

Whether you're looking for a hearty breakfast, a healthy lunch, or a satisfying dinner, we offer options for every dietary preference. Our menu includes vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free choices, ensuring everyone can enjoy a delicious meal.
