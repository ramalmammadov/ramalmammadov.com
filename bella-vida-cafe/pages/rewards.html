Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the rewards program?

Simply text BELLA777 to 578-277 and follow the link to complete your registration. You can also download the Fivestars app and search for Bella Vida Garden Cafe.

Do points expire?

No, your points never expire! You can save them up and use them whenever you'd like.

Can I use points for takeout orders?

Yes! You can earn and redeem points on dine-in, takeout, and delivery orders.

How do I check my points balance?

You can check your points balance in the Fivestars app or by texting "BALANCE" to 578-277.

When do I get my birthday rewards?

Birthday rewards are automatically added to your account during your birthday month. You'll receive a notification in the app.
