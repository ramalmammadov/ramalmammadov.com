About Us

Good Earth Organic Eatery is a family-owned, all-organic restaurant founded by Contrano Rosettani & Hilary Keever, who moved from Le Marche, Italy to Cape May in 2012.

Their food philosophy focuses on seasonal, locally-sourced ingredients, simple Italian-inspired cooking, and community well-being.

  • Stainless steel cookware (no microwaves)
  • Natural sourdough bread
  • No canned goods
"We believe in healthy people and a cleaner planet."
