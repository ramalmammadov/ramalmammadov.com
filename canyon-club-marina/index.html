Welcome to Canyon Club Resort Marina

Welcome to Canyon Club Resort Marina, Cape May's premier full-service boating facility. Located at 900 Ocean Drive, we offer a complete range of services for boaters, from dockage and fueling to repairs and supplies.

Our marina is the perfect destination for both transient and seasonal boaters, providing a welcoming atmosphere and professional service. Whether you're looking for a day trip, weekend getaway, or extended stay, Canyon Club Resort Marina is your gateway to the beautiful waters of Cape May.

With our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced staff, and commitment to excellence, we've established ourselves as the premier center for boating and its related activities in the Cape May area.

Here There Be Monsters!

Cape May is renowned for its exceptional fishing opportunities, and Canyon Club Resort Marina is your perfect base for all types of angling adventures. From inshore fishing for flounder and weakfish to offshore expeditions targeting tuna, mahi-mahi, and marlin, our location provides easy access to the best fishing grounds.

Our marina offers specialized services for fishing boats, including fish cleaning stations, bait and tackle availability, and connections to local fishing charters and guides. Whether you're a seasoned angler or just getting started, our knowledgeable staff can help you make the most of your fishing experience.

  • Engine diagnostics and repair
  • Propeller repair and replacement
  • Electrical system maintenance

Expert fiberglass repair and restoration services to keep your vessel looking great and performing optimally. Our skilled technicians handle everything from minor repairs to major restoration projects.
